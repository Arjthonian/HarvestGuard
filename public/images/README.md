# DramaticIntro Images

Place your three slide images here with the following names:

- `slide1.jpg` - First slide image
- `slide2.jpg` - Second slide image  
- `slide3.jpg` - Third slide image

The images should be:
- High quality (recommended: 1920x1080 or larger)
- Related to agriculture/harvesting/farming themes
- In JPG or PNG format

Suggested themes:
- Slide 1: Depicts the struggle of crop loss
- Slide 2: Shows food waste statistics/impact
- Slide 3: Represents hope and protection of harvest
